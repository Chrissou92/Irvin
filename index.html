CAPUTAS, HONDURAS

Located in the dry southwestern of Honduras, las
Caputas is the model of a well run Cooperative. Their official
name, Cooperativa Cafetelera Caputas Limitada or
COCAFCAL, is quite a mouthful, so we decided to pay
homage to the whole community by calling this coffee simply
"Capucas".The cooperative, run by Omar Rodriguez, utilizes
modern washing and drying facilities to produce this fantastic
Coffee.
